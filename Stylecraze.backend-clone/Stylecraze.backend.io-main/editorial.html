StyleCraze Editorial Policies & Quality Standards StyleCraze Editorial Policy

The purpose of this editorial policy is to establish a set of processes to ensure accuracy, inclusivity, objectivity, relevance, transparency, and quality of the information provided on StyleCraze.

Our Editorial Principles

  • Readers First: Every article is written and published with the reader in mind. We ensure to cover content that you are looking for to help you up your beauty, health, and wellness routines. Our language is simple, and we avoid using complex jargon.
  • Accuracy & Reliability: Our content is current, based on extensive research, and fact-checked and reviewed by members of our medical board and expert panel. We also get in touch with board-certified doctors, registered dietitians, dermatologists, and licensed estheticians to ensure you get access to expert-approved advice and hacks (if any).
  • Inclusivity & Sensitivity: Our content is primarily written for a US audience. But we make sure that our language promotes respect and inclusivity of different age groups, ethnicities, genders and sexual orientations, physical and mental abilities, and relationship statuses. The articles use empathetic, compassionate, and person-first language.

    Editorial Integrity

    The content on StyleCraze is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with your physician or a qualified healthcare provider if you have any medical condition. We make a clear distinction between editorial and advertorial content. StyleCraze team members who work on content do not work in advertising. The StyleCraze Editorial Team does not give preferential treatment to any outside resource (brand, publication, or video). In articles where an external link/resource is relevant to the topic and useful to a reader, appropriate disclosures are made in accordance with FTC guidelines. Articles in association with brands are clearly labeled ‘Sponsored’ and do not reflect the opinions of the editorial team. Read our advertising policy.
