How To Wear Red Lipstick

February 21, 2022 Written by Esha Saxena

Whenever something bold and classic comes to our mind, we think of red lipsticks. If you know how to wear red lipstick right, it can make your lips pop and look sexy and appealing. Red lips are an irreplaceable trend, and it is a universally flattering shade that compliments every skin tone. You will find endless shades of red, and you can quickly find multiple tutorials online on how to wear the shade right. If you want to rock a red lipstick look, you should know how to get the most out of it. This article will be your ultimate guide on how to wear red lipstick so that you feel bold, confident, and empowered. Read on!

How To Wear Red Lipstick - Tips And Tricks

Before we begin with our tutorial on how to wear red lipstick, here are a bunch of things to keep in mind to rock the perfect red pout.

  1. Finding your perfect red is the first hurdle. Did you know that the color red is split into various tone categories? For instance, true red, blue tone, and orange tone. Experiment with different shades to find your signature red.
  2. Next comes finding the finish and formula you’re most comfortable with. You can choose between matte, glossy, sheer, cream, and liquid lipstick.
  3. Before you begin, exfoliate your lips with a lip scrub or an old toothbrush for a smooth and flawless application. This also helps to prolong the longevity of your lipstick.
  4. Don’t forget to apply a nourishing lip balm or a primer to moisturize your lips before you set out to apply your lipstick.
  5. If you want to prevent your glossy lipstick from bleeding or feathering, use a lip pencil to line your lips before application.
  6. If you are a beginner, we recommend a shade like “Russian Red” by M.A.C. It’s right in the middle of warm and cool tones, making it the perfect red lipstick for every skin tone.

  • Lip brush

How To Wear Red Lipstick: A Step-By-Step Tutorial With Pictures

To learn how to apply red lipstick properly, follow these simple steps.

Step 1: Prep Your Lips

Youtube

Apply a nourishing lip balm, preferably before you start doing your makeup, so it has enough time to get absorbed into your skin. If your lips are chapped or dry, gently exfoliate them using a wet wipe and buff away any dead skin.

Step 2: Blot Away The Lip Balm

Youtube

Do not go in with your lipstick directly after prepping your lips. Instead, use a tissue to blot away the lip balm. Next, apply a light layer of powder on your lips with a brush to create a nice and dry canvas for your lipstick to adhere to.

Step 3: Apply The Lipstick

Youtube

Apply lipstick before you apply your lip liner. Use a lip brush to really work the formula into your lips.

Step 4: Time For Your Lip Pencil

Youtube

Use a lip pencil in a shade that matches your red lipstick to define the edges and correct any unevenness or asymmetry on the lips. You can also use the pencil to slightly (yes, just slightly) overdraw the lips to make them look poutier and fuller.

Pro Tip: Don’t use a lip liner that’s significantly lighter than your lipstick unless you want to make your lips look smaller. Also, don’t use one that’s a lot darker than your lipstick, unless you’re going for an ombre look (in which case, you must remember to blend it out really, really well).

Step 5: Blot Your Lips

Youtube

Take one ply of tissue paper and press it against your lips to absorb any excess oils and then reapply another light layer of your lipstick. This will double the wear time of your lipstick.

Step 6: Clean Up

Youtube

Use an angular brush with a little bit of concealer on it to clean up and sharpen the edges of your lips. You’re using the brush like an eraser to achieve a crispy, clean edge. Finally, take a brush and dip it in some face powder. Use it to powder the skin right along the edges of your lips.

Step 7: Check For Lipstick On Your Teeth

Youtube

When wearing red lipstick, we make the common mistake of not checking for stains on our teeth. Make sure you do a quick check before stepping out.

And you’re all set!
